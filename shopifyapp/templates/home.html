Welcome to Dee Em Auto Garage

At Dee Em Auto Garage, we are dedicated to ensuring that your vehicle receives the best care possible. Located conveniently in Meru County near the Meru Central Milk Processing Firm, our facility is designed to cater to all your automotive needs with efficiency and professionalism. Whether you’re here for routine maintenance, complex repairs, or to explore our range of spare parts, we are committed to providing exceptional services tailored to your needs. Our comprehensive range of services ensures that every aspect of your vehicle is in top condition. From Mechanical Maintenance & Repairs to Panel Beating and Spray Painting, our skilled technicians are equipped to handle a variety of tasks with precision. We also offer Computerized Diagnostics for accurate problem identification, alongside services like Tyres and Batteries replacement, Body Building, and Heavy Commercial Repairs for larger vehicles. Whatever your automotive requirements, our team is here to deliver reliable and lasting solutions. In addition to our repair and maintenance services, Dee Em Auto Garage is proud to offer a wide selection of high-quality spare parts for sale. Whether you’re looking for components to enhance your vehicle’s performance or replacements for worn-out parts, our inventory has you covered. Visit our garage to experience exceptional service, expert advice, and a customer-focused approach that sets us apart in the industry. At Dee Em Auto Garage, your satisfaction is our driving force.
